Entry & Reservations

Plan your visit with ease by checking our Opening Hours and detailed Getting Here information, including directions, parking, and public transport options. Then, browse through admission details, reserve your spot for guided tours, or book a group experience to make the most of your time at the museum. The Vincent Atelier welcomes visitors six days a week, offering generous hours to accommodate diverse schedules and preferences:

Regular Hours:

  • Tuesday to Thursday: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM (last entry at 5:30 PM)
  • Saturday to Sunday: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM (last entry at 5:30 PM)

Extended Hours:

  • Fridays: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM (last entry at 7:30 PM) for our special Late Night Art Sessions

Closed:

  • Mondays (for collection maintenance and exhibition changeovers)
  • Select public holidays (please check our website for holiday schedules)

During Late Night Art Sessions every Friday, visitors can enjoy the galleries in evening light while experiencing special programming that may include live music, artist talks, guided tours, or creative workshops. These extended hours provide perfect opportunities for after-work visits and cultural evenings in the city.

Early morning hours (8:30 AM to 10:00 AM) are available exclusively for members, researchers, and pre-booked group visits, offering a more intimate experience with the collection before public opening hours.

The Vincent Atelier is conveniently located in the cultural heart of San Lorenzo Village, Makati City, making it accessible via multiple transportation options:

By Public Transport:

  • LRT Line 1: Alight at Gil Puyat Station, then take a jeepney bound for Ayala/Washington (approximately 15-minute ride)
  • MRT: Alight at Magallanes Station, then take a jeepney marked "San Lorenzo" (approximately 10-minute ride)
  • City Bus: Routes 1, 5, and 8 stop within a 5-minute walk of our entrance

By Car:

  • From EDSA: Take the Magallanes exit toward Chino Roces Avenu
  • From Makati CBD: Head south on Arnaiz Avenue (Pasay Road)
  • Parking available in our dedicated underground facility (₱50 for the first 3 hours, ₱20 per additional hour)

By Taxi or Ride-Share:

  • Request drop-off at "The Vincent Atelier, San Lorenzo Village, Makati"
  • Dedicated ride-share pickup/drop-off zone located at the main entrance

Accessibility:

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance located at the north side of the building
  • Elevator access to all public areas
  • Accessible restrooms on all floors
  • Wheelchairs available for loan (reservation recommended)

The Vincent Atelier offers various admission options to suit different needs and interests:

General Admission:

  • Adults: ₱350
  • Seniors (60+): ₱250
  • Students (with valid ID): ₱200
  • Children (ages 7-12): ₱150
  • Children under 7: Free

Special Packages:

  • Family Pass (2 adults + up to 3 children): ₱800
  • Annual Membership: Starting at ₱2,500 (includes unlimited visits and exclusive benefits)
  • Dual Exhibition Access (when applicable): Additional ₱150

Reservation Options:

  • Walk-in visits welcome (subject to capacity)
  • Online advance booking recommended, especially for weekends and holidays
  • Priority access for online ticket holders
  • Same-day tickets available at the door (subject to availability)

Guided tours offered daily at 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM in English and Filipino (included with admission) and at 1:00 PM in other languages (reservation required).

Special exhibition tickets may require timed entry. We recommend booking these at least 3 days in advance during peak seasons.
