Director's Message

The Future is Guided by the Past

At The Vincent Atelier, history is not only preserved — it is lived, shared, and transformed. Since 1887, our journey has been defined by resilience, creativity, and a profound belief in the power of art to connect generations. Today, as we stand at the crossroads of tradition and innovation, we invite you to join us in continuing this remarkable legacy. The Vincent Atelier is more than a museum; it is a home for dreamers, creators, and storytellers who believe in the enduring spirit of human creativity.

“Art lives on through every challenge. The Vincent Atelier is here today because we believe in the power of creativity to endure and inspire.”

— Dave Mercado, Chief Executive Officer and Director of Vincent Atelier

It is with deep pride and heartfelt gratitude that I welcome you to The Vincent Atelier. As the Director, I am both humbled and inspired by the legacy entrusted to us — a legacy that has weathered revolutions, wars, and transformations, yet continues to flourish with unwavering spirit.

The Vincent Atelier began as a simple dream: a space where art and ideas could thrive. Over a century later, that dream has blossomed into a living institution that celebrates both our cultural heritage and our collective future. Every exhibit, every program, and every piece within these walls carries a story of resilience, creativity, and hope.

As we look forward, our mission is clear: to honor our roots while embracing new forms of expression that resonate with today’s world. We aim to nurture a vibrant community of artists, scholars, and visitors who will shape the next chapter of this story.

On behalf of the entire Vincent Atelier family, thank you for being part of this journey. Your presence, your curiosity, and your passion are what keep this dream alive.

Welcome to The Vincent Atelier — where the past meets possibility.
