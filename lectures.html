Lectures

Hone Your Craft

Set against the inspirational backdrop of the atelier's historic spaces, the program offers a meticulously curated selection of hands-on workshops, masterclasses, technical demonstrations, and immersive learning experiences guided by accomplished artists, master craftspeople, scholars, and cultural practitioners. These educational offerings span traditional Philippine art forms—including indigenous weaving techniques, folk pottery, ancestral woodcarving methods, and classical painting approaches—alongside contemporary practices encompassing digital media, experimental techniques, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
